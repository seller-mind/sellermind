## What each tool actually is

### SellerMind

[SellerMind](https://thesellermind.com) is an AI-first Etsy seller toolkit built around a free, credit-card-free workflow. Sellers drop in a listing or a product idea, and SellerMind returns Etsy-specific keyword suggestions, optimized titles + tags, and listing copy variations tuned for the Etsy search algorithm.

The deliberate design choice: **no recurring billing.** That means SellerMind is positioned for sellers who run a side shop, want to test before committing, or refuse to add another monthly line item to a stack that already includes Etsy fees, ads, and shipping platforms.

### Marmalead

Marmalead has been an Etsy seller staple since around 2014. The product centers on **keyword research**, with Etsy-specific metrics like "engagement" and "competition" scores, search trends, and listing grader tools. It's a paid subscription product — there's a trial but no permanent free tier on the core feature set.

Marmalead's strength is depth: long-time Etsy sellers who lean hard on data have used it for years and built workflows around its specific metrics.

### eRank

eRank is the most-installed Etsy SEO tool in the ecosystem, with both a free tier and several paid tiers. It covers a broad surface: keyword research, tag tracking, listing audits, competition tracking, shop analytics, and trend data.

eRank's strength is breadth and the free tier — it's the default "first tool" most Etsy sellers try, then either upgrade or layer specialists on top.
